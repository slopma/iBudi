# iBudi

In the realm of international business, literal or automatic (word-for-word) translations performed by generic tools like Google Translate or general AI lose the commercial and legal context. This can distort the true meaning of clauses, resulting in losses of millions of dollars or the loss of key clients.

## Value Proposition and Key Features

iBudi is designed as a keyboard-linked assistant (similar to Grammarly) or a web/mobile application with the following capabilities:

1. Contextualized Translation: Preserves the technical and business meaning in the target language.

2. Technical Term Search: By entering a business term (e.g., "International Law"), the platform displays its meaning and contextual equivalent in six major languages: Spanish, English, German, Japanese, French, and Portuguese.

3. Document and Text Analysis: Allows users to write directly or upload documents to receive recommendations for writing, spelling, and appropriateness.

4. Percentage of relevance: A metric that tells the user how appropriate and accurate the selected words are according to the context of the document.
